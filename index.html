You study hard but still get poor results. Why?

Because you're doing it wrong.

Research shows that the most commonly used study methods like rereading and highlighting are ineffective. They do little to help you remember the material you are trying to learn. They create an illusion of competence. They deceive you into thinking that you’ve mastered what you just studied, when in fact, you didn’t. These methods are a waste of your precious study time.

Better Ways to Study

There are better ways to study that will really boost your chances of passing that exam, improve your grades or master what you’re learning. The most effective learning techniques, based on hundreds of research by cognitive and educational psychologists are:

  • retrieval practice – the process of recovering knowledge and information from long-term memory, and
  • spaced repetition – distributing your study sessions over an extended period of time.

What is ioQuiz

Solving worksheets or quizzing yourself regularly requires discipline and consistency. ioQuiz is a simple web-based learning tool that helps you to be disciplined and consistent. It integrates retrieval practice and spaced repetition into your study habits by giving you short quizzes everyday. That’s it.

How it Works

You log in to your ioQuiz account each day where you have to answer a set of questions relevant to what you are trying to learn. This process continues until the end of your subscription or until you cancel. Questions will be selected and sorted according to the topics you haven’t mastered yet. You will also be able to view your history so you can see your progress.
